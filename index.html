{{ site.tagline }}

This collection presents the same study in several registers, from a long-form scholarly article to a public essay, a practice brief, and a podcast outline. The through-question: how secessionist and restorationist movements call a “people” into being, and where that rhetorical machinery holds versus where it jams. The three cases are Québec sovereignty, Alberta’s October 19, 2026 referendum, and the United States “true America” framing.
